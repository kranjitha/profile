Google Page Rank Algorithm is one of the most innovative solutions to rank web pages in a search engine. Initially, web pages were ranked based on the frequency of the key words in the search text but a spam web page can just have a single word repeated thousand to million times and be wrongfully claimed as a popular web page over a legit site which actually has useful related information. Google page rank algorithm is a graph based algorithm that treats all the webpages as nodes of a graph and the importance of a webpage is determined by what webpages point to it. Therefore the legitimacy of the popularity of webpage is given properly. Read more about google page rank algorithm here Here is how I used page rank algorithm to rank tennis players:

  • - Tennis Players cannot be ranked just based on the winning percentage because a lot of factors have to be taken into consideration. Some of the factors include: the age of match, the comparability of skills of both the tennis players, the format of match and various other factors
  • - Used Networkx to arrange players in nodes. Originally page rank algorithm gives weight as 1 to each edge. The algorithm has been tweaked a little bit to give weighted edges to give different importance to different matches based on the above mentioned factors.
    • - Another change is to have multiple edges between the nodes to represent all the matches between players (eventually all the weights of edges are combined to give one single edge.).
